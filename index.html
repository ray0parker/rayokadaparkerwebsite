About Me

Not your typical engineer.

Of course, I know about entropy, energy balances, and separation techniques. But I'm also a program manager, team leader, aerospace/mechanical engineer, drug development scientist, information technology analyst, and rocket scientist.

Currently a Senior Technical Program Manager at Oracle, driving reliability and quality programs for Oracle Cloud Analytics, Fusion Data Intelligence, and AI Data Platform. I also run an active consulting practice spanning AI/ML pipelines, RAG agents, blockchain, and product development. Past experience includes Boeing, NASA, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Orbital ATK, Northrop Grumman, Booz Allen Hamilton, Microsoft, Dragonchain, and ODEM. I hold a B.S. in Chemical Engineering from RPI (Dean's List) and an MBA from Quantic School of Business and Technology.

I'm intrigued by projects, problems, ideas, and people — seeking to inspire others and work on things that bring out the best in humanity.

During my free time I enjoy community service, reading, sports, tech and crypto, entrepreneurial projects, and motorcycle riding. Feel free to reach out!
